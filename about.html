Our story

Why I started DigMe

I started DigMe because I kept seeing the same problem: business owners who were smart, driven, and good at what they did — but who had no idea where their business stood financially. Not because they didn't care, but because their accountant only called once a year, their books were a mess, and nobody had ever explained any of it in plain language.

I'd spent years in practice watching people make avoidable mistakes — structuring their businesses wrong from the start, missing VAT registration deadlines, paying more tax than they should have because they hadn't planned properly. These weren't complex problems. They were problems that good, timely advice would have solved.

"My goal was simple: build an accounting practice that actually helps people run their businesses — not one that just files their returns and sends an invoice."

DigMe was built around cloud-first tools from day one — Xero, automated bank feeds, digital filing. Not as a gimmick, but because real-time numbers make real advice possible. When I can see what's happening in your business this month, I can help you this month. Not in March when we do your year-end.

We work primarily with South African entrepreneurs and small business owners — people who are serious about their businesses and want an accountant who's serious about them.

If that sounds like you, let's talk.

How we work

Three things we never compromise on

Cloud-first, always

Every client runs on Xero. Real-time bank feeds, automated reconciliations, live reporting. We built our practice around modern tools because they make better advice possible — not as an afterthought.

Proactive, not reactive

We don't wait for year-end to spot problems. Monthly reports, deadline tracking, and regular check-ins mean issues are flagged before they become expensive. You should never be surprised by your tax bill.

Plain language, always

No jargon, no vague answers. You get a clear explanation of what's happening, what it means for your business, and what to do about it. If you don't understand something, we haven't explained it well enough yet.

Working with DigMe

What it actually looks like

We keep things simple. No complex onboarding, no jargon-filled engagement letters, no surprises on your invoice. Here's what working with us looks like from the first call onwards.

1

A free 30-minute call

We get to know your business, what's working, what isn't, and what you actually need. No obligation — you'll leave with clarity regardless of whether you become a client.

2

We sort out the foundations

Xero setup or migration, correct chart of accounts, bank feeds connected. Usually done within a week. You start seeing clean, real-time numbers almost immediately.

3

Monthly rhythm

Books reconciled, reports delivered, deadlines tracked. You get a brief monthly summary of what's happening and a direct line for questions — not a ticket queue.

4

Ongoing advisory

As your business grows or changes, we're there. New employee? Approaching VAT threshold? Planning to raise funding? These conversations happen when they need to, not at year-end.

Every client gets a dedicated account owner who knows their numbers — backed by specialists across tax, payroll, and advisory.
